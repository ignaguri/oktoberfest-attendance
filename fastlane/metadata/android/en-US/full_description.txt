ProstCounter is the ultimate companion for Oktoberfest, the Wiesn in Munich, and beer festivals everywhere. Log every beer, every tent, every memory — then compete with your group on the leaderboard and look back on it all with your personal Wrapped.

Whether you're a Wiesn veteran or planning your first Oktoberfest 2026, ProstCounter helps you remember the days you might forget.

FEATURES
• Daily attendance log — record beers, tents and the days you showed up
• Tent tracking — find your favorites across the Theresienwiese, from Schottenhamel to Hofbräu
• Group competitions — invite friends, share a private leaderboard, see who's ahead
• Beer photos & memories — snap and save pictures from each day
• Achievements — unlock badges as you log more days, more tents, more liters
• Personal Wrapped — see your festival summary at the end of the season
• Map of the Theresienwiese — find tents, rides and your group at a glance
• Multi-festival support — works for Oktoberfest, Frühlingsfest, Cannstatter Wasen and other beer festivals
• Offline-first — log when you're in the tent, sync when you have signal again

PRIVACY
Your data stays yours. We don't sell it and we don't track you across the internet.

Download ProstCounter, gather your friends and start counting. See you at the Wiesn.
